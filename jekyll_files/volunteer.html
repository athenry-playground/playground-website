We Need Your Help

A community-owned playground relies on community care. From applying for grant funding to installing new playground equipment, repainting, and replanting, every task is essential in keeping our playground a safe and joyful place.

Join us in ensuring that the Athenry Community Playground remains a vibrant space for families and kids to enjoy for years to come. Your time and effort will make a real impact!
