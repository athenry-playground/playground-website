Our Mission

Established in 2004, Athenry Community Playground is the first recreational facility for the community at the heart of Athenry Town. For over 20 years, this beloved playground has been a central gathering spot for both locals and tourists, providing a safe, welcoming space for families and visitors of all ages.

As a cherished amenity in Athenry, the playground is showing signs of wear and tear and now requires significant restoration. We need your support to help revitalise this essential community resource.

The Athenry Community Playground Group (ACPG), run by dedicated local volunteers, oversees the development and maintenance of the playground. Every donation directly funds improvements, and we rely on the generosity of our community to keep this vital space thriving—whether through financial contributions, volunteer hours, or the donation of skills.

Our committee holds regular open meetings, inviting anyone interested in supporting the playground to get involved. If you want to learn more or are willing to volunteer your time, please contact us today. Every contribution counts, and we welcome all offers of assistance.

Together, we can ensure that Athenry Community Playground continues to be a vibrant part of our town for generations to come.
